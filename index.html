About Me

I was born at Cape Fear Hospital in North Carolina in July of 1979. Currently, I reside in Minneapolis, Minnesota and work downtown in the radio industry. Since I was eight years old, I have had a passion for computers and the art of coding. I have a ginger cat named Bruce who looks just like me. He's my best friend. Together we will take over the world. Currently, I work in radio and make commercials for a pair of radio stations. I have done this for over twenty years and have lived in five cities across the country.


My hobbies include music, video games, coding, marijuana, cats, watching wrestling, and riot multistreams.
